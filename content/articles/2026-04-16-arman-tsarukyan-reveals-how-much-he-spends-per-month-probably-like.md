# Arman Tsarukyan Reveals How Much He Spends Per Month: “Probably, Like…”

Arman Tsarukyan Reveals How Much He Spends Per Month: “Probably, Like…” APR 13, 2026BJJEE1 AdminArticles, BJJ News Arman Tsarukyan has built a reputation not just for his performances inside the cage, but also for his increasingly visible luxury lifestyle outside of it. Through social media, Tsarukyan has showcased a world of supercars, yachts, and high-end purchases… Prompting fans to wonder just how much he actually spends. Speaking on the Full Send Podcast, he offered a surprisingly candid answer: Yeah, that’s for content, as well. [How much do I spend?] If you’re not buying something, or what? If you’re buying watches and everything… I don’t know. Probably like $500 [thousand], $700 [thousand]. While he has yet to earn the massive pay bump that comes with becoming a UFC champion, Tsarukyan remains one of the wealthier fighters on the roster. His financial situation is supported not only by his fighting career, but also by his family background, with his father’s successful business ventures playing a role. A major part of his spending revolves around luxury watches: I buy, every month, one watch after my grappling or wrestling. But one watch costs like 250. If you’re gonna go there, like 200 thousand. So, it’s already [a lot]. Despite the extravagant lifestyle, Tsarukyan insists that his motivation isn’t purely financial. While many fighters chase the sport for its earning potential, his focus remains on achievement: They worry about that I’m gonna get the title and leave UFC.
